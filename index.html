About Us

We are ShareBite, a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and feeding the underprivileged. Our NGO partner, Ujjwal Bhavishya, works tirelessly to bring smiles to those in need.

Our Mission

To connect donors—restaurants, individuals, and communities—with hungry people, ensuring no meal is wasted and every life is nourished.
